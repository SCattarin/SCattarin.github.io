Born in Italy but lived in a few different countries since 2015. Although I specialized in paleontology, I'm a naturalist by heart, and the passion of photography got tangled with my studies background.

​Over the years, I developed a strong interest in the different applications of new technologies for education and culture. This brought me to work with institutions focusing on digitization, either by working on databases or by digitizing documents and museum specimens.
